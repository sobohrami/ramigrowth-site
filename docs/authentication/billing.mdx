# Billing & usage

## What you pay for

You are charged for **successful operations** — completed generations and edits. Failed jobs are not billed.

## Dashboard

Usage, per-key breakdown, and invoices are available in the RAMA dashboard (`app.rama.ai`).

## Free tier

New accounts receive a starter allocation (see current pricing page on [rama.ai](https://rama.ai)); limits reset on the published billing cycle.
